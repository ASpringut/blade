What can Blade do for me?


The Blade inventory managment system aims to make your business more agile and efficient. By automatically tracking your inventory Blade enables you to make better business decisions. With blade you can identify excess inventory in a snap, search for patterns in your customers spending and calculate pricing in a snap.

Blade is cheap and automates common tasks for you freeing up your time for the more important things.
All of your information at blade is absolutely confidential....
